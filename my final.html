My morning favourite starter

My Superfood cup of green boost


Moringa Powder is a powder made from crushed moringa leaves. It is a complete protein containing all 9 amino acid making it great choice for vegetarians.It is rich in antioxidants and other nutrients which commonly missing in the diets. Once grounded into fine powder you can it can be stored and kept for many months without refrigirator. It can be taken in the morning with your blended fruit of your choice, or add it in your plain yoghurt. It is also used for treating various other diseases, from malaria and typhoid fever to hypertension and diabetes.
